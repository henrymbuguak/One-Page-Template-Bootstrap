The Story of Our Company

H-Lab is well designed web design agency, offering the full range of web development services. H-Lab was set up in Nairobi, Kenya in 2015. From the early days we expanded into designing web sites and we were one of the first digital media business to recognise this is a key area of growth. H-Lab is making large strides in the web development and design industry. H-Lab has desire to provide affordable, high-quality website to individuals and companies. H-Lab prides itself with the user friendly and professional websites that are valuable to any audience that visits.
